ancient times, forests have been essential habitats for both humans and animals. They are homes for many different species and valuable resources. They remain important to many people. However, massive forest exploitation is taking place right now, and it's happening slowly and without warning. There are a lot of things that are to blame, but our thinking is that people's lack of awareness is a major factor. Therefore, we intend to develop a platform that not only monitors the forests but also educates, warns, and reminds individuals of their responsibility toward the forests.
